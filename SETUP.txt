SETUP INSTRUCTIONS
==================

1. HOST THE FILES
   The Power-Up must be served over HTTPS.
   Easiest options:
     a) GitHub Pages  — push the trello-powerup/ folder to a repo, enable Pages
     b) Netlify / Vercel — drag the folder into their web UI, instant HTTPS
     c) ngrok (local dev) — run: npx serve . then ngrok http 3000

2. REGISTER THE POWER-UP
   a) Go to https://trello.com/power-ups/admin
   b) Click "Create new Power-Up"
   c) Fill in:
        Name: Parent-Child Relations
        Workspace: (your workspace)
        Iframe connector URL: https://YOUR-HOST/index.html
   d) Under Capabilities, enable:
        - card-badges
        - card-back-section
   e) Save.

3. ADD TO A BOARD
   a) Open any Trello board
   b) Click Power-Ups (puzzle icon) → search "Parent-Child Relations"
   c) Click Add

4. USE IT
   Open any card. The "Parent / Children" section appears in the card back.
   - Click "Set parent" to pick a parent card from the board.
   - Click "Add child card" to assign a child.
   - The × button removes a relationship.
   - Card fronts show blue "Child of #N" and green "N children" badges.

NOTES
=====
- Relationships are stored in board-level Power-Up data (no OAuth needed).
- Circular relationships (A→B→A) are prevented automatically.
- Data persists as long as the Power-Up is enabled on the board.
- The board-level 'relations' key has a 4 KB JSON limit, enough for ~200 cards.
